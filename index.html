About Me

Dr. Bhumika Dubay is a dedicated Genomics Scientist and Postdoctoral Fellow at UAE University, with over eight years of experience across population genomics, plant molecular biology, bioinformatics, and molecular evolution. She combines computational and field-based approaches to address key questions in plant adaptation, evolutionary biology, and crop improvement.
