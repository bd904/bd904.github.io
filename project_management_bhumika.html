Project Leadership Experience

Dr. Bhumika Dubay has led and coordinated multidisciplinary research projects across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Her experience spans both academic and applied research contexts, where she has been responsible for guiding wet-lab teams, designing bioinformatics pipelines, and managing complex experimental timelines and deliverables.

Key Highlights

  • Marie Curie Fellowship (EpiDiverse): Managed genomic research across multiple partner institutions, contributing to deliverables in epigenetics, training, and collaborative data generation.
  • Prosopis Genomics at UAEU: Independently managing a full research line on adaptation genomics of arid-zone plants, balancing high-throughput sequencing, epigenetic profiling, and phenotypic analysis.
  • Supervision & Teaching: Mentored Ph.D. students during her tenure as a Teaching Assistant at Shiv Nadar University. Trained colleagues in bioinformatics tools and techniques in both formal and informal settings.
  • Cross-Institutional Collaborations: Coordinated secondments in France and Germany, bridging academic and industry approaches in genome analysis and data management.

Skills & Strengths

  • Scientific communication: conference presentations, posters, grant writing
  • Team coordination and delegation
  • Workflow optimization using computational tools
  • Project planning and reporting in EU and institutional frameworks
